# Overview

## Introduction

The Fecher AI Assistant is a prototype chatbot designed to act as a digital representative of **fecher GmbH**, an international software and consulting company.

It was built using a no-code AI platform (Chipp) and leverages structured prompt engineering combined with website-based knowledge grounding.

---

## Problem Statement

Modern B2B companies often face:

* Repetitive customer inquiries
* Slow response times
* Lack of scalable pre-sales support

This creates friction in early customer interactions and can lead to missed opportunities.

---

## Solution

The Fecher AI Assistant addresses these challenges by:

* Providing instant answers to common questions
* Structuring company knowledge into accessible responses
* Guiding users toward relevant services and contact channels

---

## Key Idea

Instead of building a traditional backend system, this project demonstrates:

👉 How **AI behavior can be controlled through prompt design**
👉 How **company knowledge can be embedded via context + retrieval**

---

## Outcome

The result is a functional AI assistant that:

* Communicates professionally in a B2B context
* Reflects the company’s services and positioning
* Operates without custom backend infrastructure

---

## Project Nature

This is a **prototype / proof-of-concept**, created to explore:

* AI-assisted business communication
* No-code AI tooling
* Prompt engineering for real-world use cases
