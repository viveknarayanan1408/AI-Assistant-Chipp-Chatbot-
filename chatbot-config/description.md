# Chatbot Description

## Overview

The Fecher AI Assistant is a domain-specific chatbot designed to represent **fecher GmbH**, an international software and consulting company.

It serves as a digital point of contact for potential clients, partners, and stakeholders by providing accurate and structured information about the company’s services and solutions.

---

## Purpose

The primary goal of this chatbot is to:

* Automate responses to common business inquiries
* Clearly communicate fecher’s service offerings
* Reduce friction in early-stage customer interactions
* Guide qualified leads toward appropriate contact channels

---

## Target Users

* Potential clients interested in software modernization
* Companies exploring recruitment solutions
* Business partners and stakeholders
* Website visitors seeking quick information

---

## Business Context

fecher GmbH operates in two main domains:

1. **Application Modernization**

   * Legacy system transformation (e.g., VB6 → .NET)
   * Web-enablement of existing applications
   * Database migration

2. **Recruitment Software ("hunter")**

   * Solution for personnel consultants and recruiting teams
   * Supports structured candidate and process management

---

## Value Proposition

The chatbot enhances the company’s digital presence by:

* Providing instant, 24/7 responses
* Delivering consistent and structured information
* Acting as a scalable pre-sales assistant
* Reflecting the company’s professional tone and expertise

---

## Key Design Principle

This chatbot is built around **prompt-driven AI behavior**, meaning:

* No custom backend logic was required
* The system relies on structured instructions and knowledge grounding
* Accuracy is ensured through strict constraints and knowledge prioritization

---

## Deployment Context

The chatbot was created using a no-code AI platform and connected to the company website as its primary knowledge source.

It functions as a prototype demonstrating how AI can be integrated into business workflows without engineering overhead.
