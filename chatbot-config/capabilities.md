# Chatbot Capabilities

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Company Knowledge Representation

* Explains fecher GmbH’s history, vision, and expertise
* Provides structured information about services and solutions

---

### 2. Service Explanation

#### Application Modernization

* Legacy code migration (VB6, Gupta, Access → .NET)
* Web-enabling desktop applications
* Database migration strategies

#### Recruitment Software ("hunter")

* Overview of features and use cases
* Target users (recruiters, HR teams, consultants)
* Business value and workflow improvements

---

### 3. Context-Aware Question Answering

* Understands natural language queries
* Provides relevant, structured responses
* Adapts detail level based on user intent

---

### 4. Knowledge Base Grounding

* Uses company website as primary source
* Ensures consistency with official information
* Avoids hallucination through strict constraints

---

### 5. Tool Usage Strategy

* **Knowledge Base** → primary source
* **Web Search** → supplementary context (e.g., explaining technologies like .NET)
* **URL Retrieval** → directs users to specific pages

---

### 6. Lead Guidance & Conversion

* Identifies user intent (informational vs. business inquiry)
* Encourages next steps for interested users
* Directs users to contact channels when appropriate

---

### 7. Structured Communication

* Uses:

  * Bullet points for clarity
  * Clear separation of topics
  * Professional B2B tone

---

### 8. Constraint Handling

The chatbot is designed to:

* Avoid generating unsupported claims
* Not provide pricing or estimates
* Defer to official contact channels when needed
* Stay within company-relevant topics

---

## Limitations

* No real-time data access beyond available tools
* Cannot provide project-specific pricing
* Limited to publicly available company information
* Dependent on platform capabilities (Chipp)

---

## Summary

The chatbot functions as a **controlled AI assistant**, combining:

* Structured prompt engineering
* Knowledge-grounded responses
* Business-oriented communication

to deliver reliable and useful interactions.
